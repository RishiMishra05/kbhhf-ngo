KarunBharat Helping Hand Foundation

Our organization is founded on principles of compassion, empathy, and community solidarity.

Services

At Karunbharat Helping Hand Foundation, we are dedicated to providing a range of vital services to those affected by cancer. Our mission is to extend a helping hand to individuals and families facing challenges during their cancer journey. Through financial aid, medical assistance, accommodation support, educational initiatives, employment assistance, community building, and engaging fundraising events, we strive to make a meaningful impact on the lives of those in need. Join us in our commitment to creating a compassionate and supportive community.

Financial Assistance:

Emergency financial aid for families affected by cancer.Direct monetary assistance to the most vulnerable families for their treatment and rehabilitation.

Medical Assistance:

Emergency medical assistance for critical situations.Financial support for necessary medical treatments.

Accomodation Support:

Arranging suitable accommodation for underprivileged patients during their treatment.

Educational Support

Providing monthly rations and covering children's educational expenses for cancer-affected individuals.

Employement Assistance

Assisting family members in finding employment if the primary breadwinner loses the battle against cancer.

Community Building

Creating a community of 1000 members who contribute a minimum affordable amount towards the well-being of cancer-affected families.

Fundraising Event

Organizing fundraising events, such as live musical shows, to promote a culture of charity.
